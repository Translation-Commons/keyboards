Start Using kirikirʔi:s

kirikirʔi:s (Wichita) Spoken in Anadarko, Oklahoma

Keyboard Layout

Desktop

High tone vowels

High tone vowels [á, é, í, ó, Á, É, Í, Ó] are entered by typing a vowel followed by a backtick [`].

Long vowels

Vowel length is indicated by entering a vowel followed by one or two colons [a:, a::, e:, e::, i:, i::, o:, o::, A:, A::, E:, E::, I:, I::, O:, O::]. There is no special keystroke or combination for this..

Glottal stop

The glottal stop [ʔ] is entered by typing two question marks [??].

Mobile

High tone vowels and long vowels/h3>

High tone vowels and long vowels are entered by long-pressing a vowel and selecting from the sub-menu.

Glottal stop

The glottal stop [ʔ] is placed to the right of the [L] key.
